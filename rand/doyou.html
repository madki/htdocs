Do you see her?

Stunned to a stop on that marvelous night,
Utterly spellbound by the presence ahead,
My mind trying to warp time, unwilling to depart,
Even when she was ushering me forward,
Dulling all other senses to enhance my sight,
Hoping to hold the complete memory of the moment,
Admiring with obvious amazement, I see her.

She was a breathtaking beauty,
Yet with such simplicity.
And I held my stare grounded,
Motionless and astounded.
